What is required to get a cross approved?

  • It must be clear that you have completed the task and interpreted it correctly.
  • You must have at least 2 witnesses or have filmed / taken a picture that the task is completed. Pictures and videos are sent in the group 'BINGO' underway.
  • For tasks that may be difficult to document, you must get approval from at least 4 other participants.
  • To be able to cross off a 'JOKER', all the other participants must approve it.
  • If something is unclear / disputes arise, the person who created the task will decide the outcome.
  • A task cannot be done within another task. For example, you cannot cross off 'kiss a Lars' and 'kiss someone of the opposite gender' for the same kiss.
  • If you are caught cheating, you must Vipps 50 NOK to the person who created the task. At the same time, that person can post a random post on one of your social media.
